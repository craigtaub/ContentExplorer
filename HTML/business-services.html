Business Services

When you are too busy trying to do everything to promote yourself and get the orders in, take on new employees or deal with the latest legislation, it seems too much to start writing things down into formal guidance, processes or training manuals.  However, over time these are the things you need to do to ensure that your business can survive and continue if something unexpected happens, if you are faced with an audit, if you need to induct and train new staff or you want to apply for funding to expand your business or project.

The services that I can provide will assist you in making sure that the foundations that you have worked so hard in building a successful business or project are solid and sound and are there for you to confidently explain, demonstrate or defend. 

Business Process Mapping - even the simplest concepts and topics can be difficult to explain if all you have is the knowledge and experience in your head or only in a few Word documents, especially if you have to explain it under pressure.  In the fast-paced world we live in now most senior managers, clients or potential partners do not have the time to read through large volumes of information on what you do.  They want to know in a one page document or five minute presentation what you can offer or what your processes are.

You will gain more credibility if you and your employees can explain what you do accurately and succinctly.  By having a flow chart or diagram that explains what you do saves time and is likely to demonstrate confidence in you as an employer, service provider, project/funding manager or business person.

Through detailed interviews, design and clarification reviews, I will work with you to ensure that all the knowledge that you have built up is transferred from your head to paper or interactive electronic medium.  Through this process, I can also suggest areas where improvements can be made through further detailed guidance, checklists or the introduction or improvement of management and control processes.

Document Services - with the internet being used to research your business or projects the appearance and content of your website are paramount.  First impressions count and that also goes for your documentation whether it is your training manuals, policies, blog posts or articles.  The sometimes careless misplacing of an apostrophe or the wrong use or spelling of a word or name could mean the loss of potential business or contracts, especially if your main work is providing reports or documents. 

With an eye for detail and a creative edge, I can help you with the following document services:
  • Policy Document Drafting
  • Formatting and Proofreading

Monitoring reports - do you manage a funded project that requires you to complete regular monitoring reports to your funders?  If so, I can take the hassle out of the drafting of it for you.  With experience of analysing monitoring reports from a funder’s perspective, we know what is normally required and how to present it.

Independent review of funding applications - if you’ve ever applied for funding before, you’ll be aware that funders are looking for specific things in terms of
  • How will the funding add value to the project?
  • Is there a definite well-researched need for the project?
  • Does your organisation have the capacity to undertake and deliver a successful project and manage the funding properly?
By applying all of the document services above, I can help by undertaking an independent review of any applications that you have put together and provide feedback on the content and structure.
